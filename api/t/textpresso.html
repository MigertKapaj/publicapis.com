Textpresso

Textpresso is an information extracting and processing package for biological literature. It was initially developed by Hans-Michael Muller, Eimear Kenny and Paul W. Sternberg, with contributions from Juancarlos Chan and David Chen. This new version (officially known as Textpresso 2.0) was developed by Hans-Michael Muller with contributions from Arun Rangarajan and Tracy K. Teal.
