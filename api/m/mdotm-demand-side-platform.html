MdotM Demand Side Platform

Welcome to the MdotM Demand Side Platform API! The MdotM API allows MdotM advertisers to programmatically: Track conversion events (mobile web, app installs, and post-install activity) Get account history, campaign performance data, and conversion events by DeviceID, Get audience statistics (by geo, device, OS), Update campaign parameters (bids, targeting, caps)
